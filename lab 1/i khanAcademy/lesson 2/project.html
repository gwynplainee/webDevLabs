What to do?

  1. Skiing at the Shymbulak Mountain Resort

    They say that the best snowboarders and skiers after death get to Almaty... Of course, in a city that is close to the mountains there can't be no ski resorts.
    Here everyone will find a track to his liking - soft gentle slopes for beginners, high-speed slopes for professionals, ski jumps for stunt skiers.
    Various complexes will allow you to admire beautiful views and enjoy the stunning nature.

  2. Horse rides

    In today's stressful world, horseback riding tours are a way to really enjoy and relax. There is even such a type of rehabilitation as hippotherapy.
    There is definitely no shortage of offers to ride horses in Almaty. After all, the descendants of nomads are riders ‘at the genetic level’,
    and the climate and natural landscapes only favour the development of this direction.

Almaty is a city not only for cool entertainment, but also for cultural pastime.
In this case, the city offers a lot of entertainment that can surprise every visitor and resident.
Besides, Almaty is well known as one of the historical places of Kazakhstan, as the main settlement in its area was founded in the Bronze Age.
Having said that, the number of cultural places such as museums and theatres is diverse.
