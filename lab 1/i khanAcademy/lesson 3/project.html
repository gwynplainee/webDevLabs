Mac & Cheese

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 2 cups
Butter 1/4 cup
Flour 1/4 cup
Milk 1,5 cup
Heavy cream 1/2 cup
Cheddar cheese 1,5 cup
Parmesan cheese 1,5 cup

Step 1: Boil pasta.
Step 2: Melt butter, add seasonings and flour.
Step 3: Slowly whisk in milk, cream.
Step 4: Reduce the heat to low and add the cheeses.
Step 5: Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and stir to coat.

Beef Stir-Fry

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Rice noodles 8 ounces
Beef sirloin 1 pound
Broccoli florets 1 cup
Bell pepper 1
Carrot 1
Green onion 2
Garlic cloves 2
Soy sauce 1/4 cup
Oyster sauce 2 tablespoons
Sesame oil 1 tablespoon
Sugar 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Boil noodles.
Step 2: Whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar.
Step 3: Cook the sliced beef util browned and cooked through and set aside.
Step 4: Fry the broccoli, bell pepper, carrot, gerlic.
Step 5: Return the beef add the noodles, pour the sauce over everything.
Step 6: Toss to coat everything then add sliced onions. Serve hot.

Beef Caldareta

  • Time: 4 hours
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Potatoes 3
Beef chuck 2.5 pounds
Tomato paste 2 tablespoons
Bell pepper 2
Carrot 2
Onion 1
Garlic cloves 6
Beef stock 2 cups
Tomatoes 14 ounces
Soy sauce 1 tablespoon
Fish sauce 1 tablespoon
Oyster sauce 1 tablespoon
Sugar 2 tablespoon

Step 1: Heat heavy pot with oil on high and sear beef, so all sides are browned.
Step 2: Remove beef, add onions, garlic,tomato paste. Then add crushed tomatoes and mix until prepared.
Step 3: Add back the beef and cover with beef stock. Season with fish sauce, soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar.
Step 4: Bring to simmer and cook low and slow for 3 hours with the lid.
Step 5: Add fried carrots and potatoes, bell peppers in the last 20 minutes.
Step 6: Boil the rice.Serve with hot rice and enjoy!.
